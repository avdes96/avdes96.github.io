The London Underground

The London Underground, or the Tube, is an underground rail network which spans London. It makes is easy to get around London!

Photo credit to Markus Freise, via unsplash

The Tube consists of various different lines, which includes:

  • The Central Line
  • The Victoria Line
  • The Picadilly Line
  • The Jubilee Line
  • The Northern Line
  • The District Line
  • The Circle Line
  • The Hammersmith and City Line
